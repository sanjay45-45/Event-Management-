About The Event

The sanjay tech in 2024 is the premier event for technology professionals, entrepreneurs, and innovators from around the globe. This year's theme is "The Future of Innovation: Building Tomorrow's World Today."

Over three exciting days, you'll have the opportunity to learn from industry leaders, participate in hands-on workshops, and network with like-minded professionals who are shaping the future of technology.
